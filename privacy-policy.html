Privacy Policy for CarMate

Last updated: May 04, 2026

CarMate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by CarMate.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, phone number, and location data when you use the app for carpooling services.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including ride matching, GPS tracking, and safety features like the SOS emergency system.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to provide the carpooling service or as required by law (e.g., IT Act Section 79 compliance).

4. Data Deletion

Users can request the deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@rideon.app or through the app settings.

5. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All data is encrypted in transit.
