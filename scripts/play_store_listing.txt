--- FULL DESCRIPTION ---
RideOn - India's Smart Car Pooling App

Kahan jaana hai? RideOn dhoondh leta hai!

Chahe aapko office jaana ho, ghar wapas aana ho, ya kisi doosre shahar ka safar karna ho - RideOn aapko apne raaste mein jaane wale logon ke saath carpool karne deta hai. Sasta, surakshit aur seedha.

--- APP KYA KARTA HAI? ---

 1. RIDE BOOK KARO
    • Kahan se kahan jaana hai - bas search karo
    • Available rides dikhengi apne aap
    • Ek tap mein seat book karo
    • Driver se seedha chat karo

 2. RIDE PUBLISH KARO
    • Khud carpool banayo
    • Apna route, time aur price set karo
    • Passengers ko booking karte dekho
    • Earning + company - dono!

 3. REAL-TIME CHAT
    • Driver aur passenger aapas mein chat kar sakte hain
    • Pickup point, time sab discuss karo
    • Bina call kiye hi baat ho jaati hai

 4. SURAKSHA (SOS)
    • Emergency mein ek button dabao - SOS alert jaayega
    • Admin turant dekh lega

 5. DOCUMENT VERIFICATION
    • Sab drivers verified hote hain
    • Driving license aur gaadi papers upload karne padte hain
    • Admin approve karta hai - phir hi driver banoge

 6. REVIEWS & RATINGS
    • Har ride ke baad rating do
    • Achhe drivers ko upar aate dekho

--- FEATURES ---
• GPS-based smart search - aapke paas se guzarne wali rides milegi
• Pro-rata pricing - jitna safar utna paisa, fair deal
• Bilingual - English aur Hindi
• Dark Mode - raat ko aankhon par zor nahi
• Real-time notifications - booking, cancellation, message sab turant pata chale
• Secure payments - app ke andar hi sab hota hai
• Admin verified drivers - bharosa ka app

--- ANUJNYA (PERMISSIONS) ---
RideOn sirf zaroori permissions maangta hai:
• Location - GPS search aur map ke liye
• Camera - profile photo aur document upload ke liye
• Internet - rides dhoondhne aur chat ke liye
Hum kabhi bhi aapka data bechte nahi.
