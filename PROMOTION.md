## How It Works

### Passengers के लिए:
1. Search ride (From → To → Date)
2. Choose ride aur book seat
3. Pay driver directly

### Drivers के लिए:
1. Publish ride (From → To → Time)
2. Earn money aur fuel cost share करें
3. Safe passengers को destination तक deliver करें
